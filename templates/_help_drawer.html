First Time Here?

Step 1 – Create or link an account

Head to the Accounts page.

If you don’t have an execution account yet, click Create account to open a new account with the broker.

If you already have an account, click Link account, select your MT4/MT5 server, and enter your account number and master password to connect.

Step 2 – Start copying a strategy

On the Dashboard, select your execution account, scroll to the bottom, and click Copy strategy. Choose your trade sizing and preferred copy method to begin.

Step 3 – Manage your copying

Open the Copying tab to view all active strategies and their copy settings. You can edit settings or stop copying at any time.

Step 4 – View trades

Go to Copier Trades, select an account, and view all open and recent trades for that account.
